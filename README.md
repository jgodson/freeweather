# freeweather
Mobile app (For Android &amp; iOS) created with PhoneGap to provide local weather info

<h3>You can see the app store listings at the following links</h3>
<ul>
  <li><h3><a href="https://itunes.apple.com/WebObjects/MZStore.woa/wa/viewSoftware?id=1163834276&mt=8">iOS</a></h3></li>
  <li><h3><a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.jasongodson.freeweather">Android</a></h3></li>
</ul>

<h3>Current Features V2.0.1</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Uses Device GPS to get location</li>
  <li>Uses location information to get current weather and display it</li>
  <li>Shows a visual representation of day/night progress in relation to sunrise or sunset</li>
  <li>Shows cloud cover, wind speed and direction, barometric pressure and humidity</li>
  <li>Gives a summary of the current day and two days into the future based on the data</li>
  <li>Able to graph temperature and precipitation for your choice of one, three or five days</li>
  <li>Also displays 5 days of upcoming weather in 3 hour increments, grouped by day</li>
  <li>Background changes from a light color during the day to a darker color after sunset</li>
  <li>Able to turn off dynamic background and use a plain greyscale background</li>
</ul>
